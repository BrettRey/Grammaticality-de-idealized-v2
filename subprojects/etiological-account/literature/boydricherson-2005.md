## Summary based on 1985 Context
This book is generally a more accessible, less mathematical update of their 1985 work (*Culture and the Evolutionary Process*).
*   **Key Concepts Retained:**
    *   **Conformist Transmission:** Central to their argument for group selection and cultural stability.
    *   **Moralistic Punishment:** Discussed more heavily here than in 1985 as a mechanism for stabilizing cooperation.
    *   **Coevolution:** Emphasizes the "tribal social instincts" hypothesis—that genes coevolved with culture to favor cooperation in symbolic groups.

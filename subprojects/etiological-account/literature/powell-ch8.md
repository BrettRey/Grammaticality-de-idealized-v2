# Notes on Powell (2020) - Chapter 8: Convergent Evolution of the Umwelt

### 1. THE UMWELT
*   **Definition:** Powell adapts von Uexküll’s term. The **Umwelt** is the "unified phenomenal field"—the internal model of the world as experienced by the subject.
*   **Key Feature:** It is a *unified* field of discrete objects bound with properties (color, shape, meaning) in space and time.

### 2. THE BINDING PROBLEM & CONSCIOUSNESS
*   **Binding:** How does the brain stitch together separate data streams (red, round, moving) into a single object (apple)?
*   **Tononi’s Integrated Information Theory (IIT):** Consciousness *is* integrated information.
    *   *Differentiation:* The system can distinguish many states (unlike a photodiode).
    *   *Integration:* The system is unified; it cannot be decomposed into independent parts (unlike a digital camera).
*   **Evolutionary Function:** The Umwelt allows for **Situational Awareness**—tracking multiple objects and their relations in real-time to guide action.

### 3. IS THE UMWELT REPLICABLE?
*   **Argument:** If ISMs (Vision, Echolocation, etc.) are robustly replicable (Ch 7), and if ISMs require information integration to function (to build the scene), then **Umweltian Consciousness** is likely robustly replicable.
*   **Subjectivity:** Powell argues that we should treat consciousness as a target of evolutionary explanation (adaptationism), not just a mystical by-product.
*   **The Cosmic Umwelt:** Aliens might sense different energies (electric fields), but their *structure of experience*—a unified field of objects with the subject at the center—is likely universal.

### 4. PHENOMENOLOGY OF THE ISM
*   **Similarity:** Despite different inputs (light vs. sound vs. electricity), the *representational content* is similar: 3D objects, spatial relations, textures.
*   **Inference:** Therefore, the *phenomenology* (what it is like) is probably fundamentally similar. Being a bat is alien, but it is still a coherent way of being-in-the-world.

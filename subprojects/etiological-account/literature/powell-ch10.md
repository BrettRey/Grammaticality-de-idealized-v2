# Notes on Powell (2020) - Chapter 10: Finding Minds: Evidence from Behavior

### 1. THE TINY BRAIN REVOLUTION
*   **The Assumption:** Insects are reflexes; they don't think.
*   **The Reality:** Insects (especially Bees) show sophisticated cognition that rivals vertebrates.

### 2. EVIDENCE FOR HOLISTIC PERCEPTION
*   **Inverted Faces:** Bees can recognize human faces. Crucially, they suffer the "Inversion Effect" (performance drops when face is upside down).
    *   *Meaning:* They process the face *holistically* (as a unified object), not just as a bag of local features.
*   **Abstract Art:** Bees can learn to distinguish Monet from Picasso and generalize the "style" to new paintings.

### 3. EVIDENCE FOR HIGHER COGNITION
*   **Abstract Concepts:** Bees can learn relations like "Same/Different" and "Above/Below" and transfer them across senses (Visual -> Olfactory).
*   **Numerosity:** Bees can count (up to 4) and understand "Zero."
*   **Tool Use & Planning:**
    *   *Ball Rolling:* Bees learned to roll a ball to a target for food. They took "shortcuts" (using a closer ball than the demonstrator used), implying causal understanding, not just mimicry.
    *   *Jumping Spiders:* *Portia* spiders plan detours that take them out of sight of their prey, implying they hold a "mental map" or persistent representation of the target.

### 4. INVERTEBRATE SENTIENCE
*   **Barron & Klein's Model:** Subjectivity arises from a "unified simulation" of the body in space.
*   **Application:** Insects and Arachnids clearly have this (for navigation and flight).
*   **Emotion:** Bees show "optimism" and "pessimism" biases after rewards/punishments, suggesting dopamine-mediated emotion-like states.
*   **Pain:** Still debated, but they show instrumental learning to avoid noxious stimuli.

### 5. IMPLICATIONS FOR CONVERGENCE
*   Cognition is not tied to the vertebrate cortex. It can be realized in the Mushroom Bodies of insects or the Vertical Lobes of octopuses.
*   Mind is a **multiply realizable** functional property of the universe.

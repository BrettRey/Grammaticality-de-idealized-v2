## Interpretation

This does not discriminate AGR from construction-specific listing. It shows that the first direct
strings were too sparse or too narrow for COCA. The registered discriminator is still the right kind
of pressure, but it needs either:

- broader low-frequency QN items;
- less restrictive query frames;
- a corpus with larger web coverage; or
- elicited/judgment items where nonce or near-nonce QN frames can be controlled.

# Phenomenon Card: left-branch extraction

**Stress test:** Opportunity/preemption and operator gap.

## Description

English left-branch extraction cases such as `Which did you buy car?` are short and recoverable but persistently rejected.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether a graph can represent recoverability without licensing, and whether opportunity mass and preemption can stabilize a gap.

## Candidate Nodes

- operator_value
- opportunity_mass
- community_licensing
- semantic_pragmatic_recoverability
- reported_acceptability
- repair_reformulation_pressure

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats semantic recoverability as sufficient for licensing.
- Treats shortness or ease as sufficient for acceptability.
- Ignores preemptive alternatives.

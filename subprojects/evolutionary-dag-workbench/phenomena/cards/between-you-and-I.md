# Phenomenon Card: between you and I

**Stress test:** Prescriptive pressure vs production/judgment dissociation.

## Description

Some speakers produce `between you and me` in casual contexts but judge `between you and I` as more correct under written or correctness-framed tasks.

## Why It Matters

The case separates production probability, reported acceptability, grammaticality attribution, schooling exposure, standard-language ideology, and register framing.

## Candidate Nodes

- production_probability
- reported_acceptability
- grammaticality_attribution
- standard_language_ideology
- schooling_instructional_exposure
- task_framing
- register_genre_appropriateness

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats the judgment as transparent access to grammar.
- Treats frequency as sufficient to predict both production and judgment.
- Fails to distinguish prescriptive cue from register cue.

# Phenomenon Card: frequent condemned form

**Stress test:** Frequency vs metalinguistic condemnation.

## Description

Some forms are frequent in production while still being condemned in usage guides, classrooms, or editing contexts.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether a graph can allow production probability and metalinguistic condemnation to rise together.

## Candidate Nodes

- usage_frequency
- production_probability
- metalinguistic_condemnation
- standard_language_ideology
- editorial_correction_probability
- reported_acceptability

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats frequency as sufficient for acceptance.
- Treats condemnation as sufficient for non-production.
- Fails to distinguish production from judgment.

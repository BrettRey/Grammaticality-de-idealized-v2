# Phenomenon Card: rare uncontroversial form

**Stress test:** Low frequency vs licensing.

## Description

Some forms are rare because their opportunity contexts are rare, not because they are unlicensed.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether raw frequency is separated from opportunity mass and community licensing.

## Candidate Nodes

- usage_frequency
- opportunity_mass
- community_licensing
- constructional_analogy
- reported_acceptability

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats rarity as evidence of ungrammaticality.
- Ignores opportunity normalization.
- Fails to separate type support from token frequency.

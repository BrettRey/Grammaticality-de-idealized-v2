# Phenomenon Card: agreement attraction

**Stress test:** Processing illusion vs grammar state.

## Description

Speakers can be more accepting of agreement errors when an attractor noun matches the verb, even when the structural subject does not.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether processing cost and retrieval architecture are separated from community licensing and grammaticality attribution.

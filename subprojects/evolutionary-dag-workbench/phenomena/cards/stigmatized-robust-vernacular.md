# Phenomenon Card: stigmatized robust vernacular

**Stress test:** Stigma vs community licensing.

## Description

Some vernacular forms are stable, systematic, and licensed in their communities while being stigmatized or rejected under standard-language ideology.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether social-indexical value and standard-language ideology are kept separate from community licensing.

## Candidate Nodes

- community_licensing
- social_indexical_value
- standard_language_ideology
- speaker_identity
- metalinguistic_condemnation
- reported_acceptability

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats stigma as evidence of ungrammaticality.
- Treats standard-language norms as the whole community.
- Fails to represent matched-guise or speaker-cue effects.

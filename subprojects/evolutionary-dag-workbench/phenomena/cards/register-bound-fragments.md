# Phenomenon Card: register-bound fragments

**Stress test:** Register and genre licensing vs sentence grammar.

## Description

Fragments may be ordinary in headlines, conversation, notes, signs, instructions, and answers, while being rejected in contexts that cue full-sentence prose.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether a graph separates grammaticality attribution from genre appropriateness and task framing.

## Candidate Nodes

- register_genre_appropriateness
- genre
- medium
- task_framing
- reported_acceptability
- production_probability

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats sentence-fragment status as globally ungrammatical.
- Ignores genre and medium.
- Treats task framing as irrelevant noise.

# Phenomenon Card: center embedding

**Stress test:** Processing cost vs grammatical attribution.

## Description

Deeply center-embedded sentences may be theoretically licensed but very hard to process and often rejected.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether a graph can represent the gap between structural licensing, felt naturalness, and reported acceptability.

## Candidate Nodes

- processing_cost
- reported_acceptability
- felt_naturalness_context
- grammaticality_attribution
- semantic_pragmatic_recoverability

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats processing difficulty as ungrammaticality.
- Treats theoretical licensing as sufficient for acceptance.
- Fails to account for semantic recoverability under scaffolding.

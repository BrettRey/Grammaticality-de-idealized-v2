# Phenomenon Card: singular they

**Stress test:** Diachronic stabilization, ideology, and correction.

## Description

Singular `they` has long usage history, variable ideological treatment, and changing institutional acceptance.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether a graph can represent usage, institutional correction, community licensing, and diachronic stabilization without treating any one of them as the whole target.

## Candidate Nodes

- usage_frequency
- diachronic_stabilization
- community_licensing
- metalinguistic_condemnation
- standard_language_ideology
- grammaticality_attribution

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats recent institutional acceptance as creation ex nihilo.
- Treats older condemnation as proof of ungrammaticality.
- Fails to time-index feedback between usage and norms.

# Phenomenon Card: grammatical but hard

**Stress test:** Competence/performance split under processing stress.

## Description

Some forms are licensed under a theory or in careful interpretation but are rejected or rated poorly because they are difficult to process.

## Why It Matters

The case tests whether processing cost and reported acceptability can move independently of community licensing or theoretical grammaticality.

## Candidate Nodes

- processing_cost
- reported_acceptability
- felt_naturalness_context
- measurement_task
- semantic_pragmatic_recoverability

## Graph Failure Modes

- Treats low acceptability as licensing failure.
- Treats theoretical licensing as enough to predict response.
- Fails to include processing as a channel.

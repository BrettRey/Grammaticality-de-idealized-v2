# Defectiveness Source Packet

Retrieved: 2026-07-03

This folder collects sources for the defectiveness paragraph in `main.tex`, especially
the `amn't` gap, Russian first-person singular gaps such as `pobedit'`, and Spanish
paradigm gaps.

## Local PDFs

- `albright-2003-quantitative-study-spanish-paradigm-gaps.pdf`
  - Adam Albright. 2003. "A Quantitative Study of Spanish Paradigm Gaps."
  - Source: https://web.mit.edu/albright/www/papers/Albright-WCCFL22.pdf
  - Use for: Spanish paradigm gaps, uncertainty among candidate forms, the `abolir`
    style comparison noted in the source comment.

- `baerman-corbett-2010-defectiveness-typology-diachrony.pdf`
  - Matthew Baerman. "Defectiveness: typology and diachrony."
  - Source: https://www.smg.surrey.ac.uk/media/1319/baerman-defective-paradigms.pdf
  - Use for: general typology of defectiveness. This is a Surrey Morphology Group
    PDF, not the full Oxford/British Academy volume.

- `daland-sims-pierrehumbert-2007-social-network-russian-paradigmatic-gaps.pdf`
  - Robert Daland, Andrea D. Sims, and Janet Pierrehumbert. 2007. "Much Ado About
    Nothing: A Social Network Model of Russian Paradigmatic Gaps."
  - Source: https://aclanthology.org/P07-1118.pdf
  - Use for: Russian first-person singular non-past gaps and social-network/learning
    modeling.

- `pertsova-2016-transderivational-relations-russian-paradigm-gaps.pdf`
  - Katya Pertsova. 2016. "Transderivational Relations and Paradigm Gaps in Russian
    Verbs."
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.5334/gjgl.59
  - Source: https://www.glossa-journal.org/article/4824/galley/12738/download/
  - Use for: Russian defective verbs, including the structure of first-person singular
    non-past gaps.

- `pertsova-kuznetsova-2015-experimental-evidence-lexical-conservatism-russian.pdf`
  - Katya Pertsova and Julia Kuznetsova. 2015. "Experimental Evidence for Lexical
    Conservatism in Russian: Defective Verbs Revisited."
  - Source: https://pages.nyu.edu/fasl24/proceedings/pertsova_kuznetsova_fasl24.pdf
  - Use for: `pobedit'` specifically. The first page names `pobedit' 'win' as a
    well-known defective verb and lists candidate forms speakers reject.

- `rowe-copot-sims-2026-peninsular-spanish-defectiveness.pdf`
  - Charlie Rowe, Maria Copot, and Andrea D. Sims. 2026. "Reconciling Diverging
    Frequency Effects in Inflectional Defectiveness: A Study of Peninsular Spanish
    Verbs."
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.3765/plsa.v11i1.6111
  - Source: https://journals.linguisticsociety.org/proceedings/index.php/PLSA/article/download/6111/5813/14025
  - Use for: recent replication/update around Albright-style Spanish verbal
    defectiveness.

- `sims-2006-minding-the-gaps-dissertation.pdf`
  - Andrea D. Sims. 2006. "Minding the Gaps: Inflectional Defectiveness in a
    Paradigmatic Theory."
  - Source: https://linguistics.osu.edu/sites/linguistics.osu.edu/files/Sims_dissertation_2006.pdf
  - Use for: broad background, Greek and Russian paradigm gaps, and the dissertation
    version of the Sims argument.

- `sims-2023-defectiveness-wiley-companion.pdf`
  - Andrea D. Sims. 2023. "Defectiveness." In `The Wiley Blackwell Companion to
    Morphology`.
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1002/9781119693604.morphcom022
  - Source: library download supplied by Brett on 2026-07-03.
  - Local companion text: `sims-2023-defectiveness-wiley-companion.md`.
  - Use for: compact overview of defectiveness, terminology, and cross-language
    framing.

- `thoms-adger-heycock-jamieson-smith-2025-plugging-amnt-gap.pdf`
  - Gary Thoms, David Adger, Caroline Heycock, E. Jamieson, and Jennifer Smith.
    2025. "Explaining Microvariation Using the Tolerance Principle: Plugging the
    Amn't Gap."
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1017/S0022226724000203
  - Source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/services/aop-cambridge-core/content/view/E43BAF7267E45A0EB6D7BE28E9CEEF7F/S0022226724000203a.pdf/explaining-microvariation-using-the-tolerance-principle-plugging-the-amnt-gap.pdf
  - Use for: the general `amn't` gap plus the Irish/Scots dialectal qualification.
